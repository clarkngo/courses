## Hydraulic Hose Applications

Gates hydraulic hoses are designed for a world of applications, organized by pressure range.

---

## Extremely High-Pressure Hoses

Reinforced with 4 or 6 spiral wires for optimum performance in severe conditions such as hydrostatic transmissions and high-impulse off-road equipment.

| Hose | Pressure | SAE Spec | Key Uses |
|------|----------|----------|----------|
| **EFG6K** | 6,000 psi | SAE 100R15 | Snow groomers, blacktop milling, off-road construction |
| **EFG5K** | 5,000 psi | SAE 100R13 | High-impulse off-road construction equipment |
| **EFG4K** | 4,000 psi | SAE 100R11/12 | Very high-pressure construction and mining |
| **EFG3K** | 3,000 psi | SAE 100R11/12 | High-pressure general purpose |

---

## Very High-Pressure Hoses

Reinforced with 4 spiral wires; meet governmental agency requirements. Used in **construction**, **mining**, and **heavy equipment**.

- **EFG4K (C12M)** — meets/exceeds SAE 100R12; very flexible, bends to ½ the bend radius of SAE 100R12.

---

## High-Pressure Hoses

The most common pressure range. These hoses appear on nearly every type of mobile machinery.

| Hose | Key Feature | Common Uses |
|------|-------------|-------------|
| **G2** | Most popular hose on the market | Backhoes, forklifts, trash trucks, agricultural & construction equipment |
| **M2T (MegaFlex)** | ½ bend radius of G2; SAE 100R16 | Superior impulse performance; lightweight; tighter bends |
| **M3K** | 3,000 psi all sizes | Farm equipment; economical alternative to G2 |
| **J2AT ("jack" hose)** | 10,000 psi | Porta-Power units; body shops; fire departments |

## Medium-Pressure Hoses

Used on trucks, buses, and industrial equipment. Each designed for a specific fluid or environment.

| Hose | Key Fluid/Use |
|------|---------------|
| **G1** | Agricultural, snowplows, medium-pressure oil lines |
| **C3H** | Hydraulic oil, antifreeze, water, return lines, car wash wand |
| **PowerClean™ 3000** | Hot and cold pressure washer applications |
| **GTH (C6H)** | Hydraulic oil, automatic transmission cooler lines |
| **GT7 / GT7NC** | Petroleum, water emulsion, synthetic fluids; GT7NC has non-conductive orange cover for aerial lifts |
| **GT7DL / GT7DLNC** | Dual hose (two molded together); forklifts and snowplows |
| **GT8 / GT8NC** | High-pressure lines; GT8NC non-conductive for aerial lifts |

### Medium-Pressure Fleet Hoses

The Heavy Duty Fleet industry replaced copper tubing with rubber hose. Because copper tubing is measured by OD, a **-8C5 hose and -8 copper tubing share the same ID of 13/32"**.

| Hose | Key Feature / Uses |
|------|--------------------|
| **C5C** | SAE 100R5; air, lube oil, petroleum, air brake lines, power steering (HD commercial only) — **not** for gasoline or diesel |
| **C5D** | CPE tube; handles phosphate ester fluids; air, oil, air brake, tilt cab |
| **C5M** | Marine — diesel fuel, gasoline; **Coast Guard approved** |
| **C5E** | Higher temperature; power steering, hot lube, fuel filters, coolant |
| **LP350** | Liquid or gaseous propane, butane, or combinations |
| **C14 (PTFE)** | High temperature +400°F; air brake compressor, hot oil, gasoline |
| **PolarSeal®** | Refrigerant R134A, R12, R22; automotive and truck A/C |
| **Thermoplastic** | Forklifts, snowplows, aerial lifts; lightweight, high-visibility |

> ⚠️ **PTFE Electrostatic Warning:** Many PTFE applications do not require a conductive tube, but always review the potential for electrostatic discharge. In those cases, use Gates **C14CT** (conductive PTFE) hose.

## Low-Pressure / Suction / Return Lines

Used where working pressures are under 300 psi. Handle hydraulic oil, antifreeze, water, air, and diesel fuels.

| Hose | Key Feature |
|------|-------------|
| **GMV — Global MegaVac®** | Double-spiral helical wire prevents collapse; ½ bend radius of SAE 100R4; return line and suction hose |
| **RLA** | 1-fiber braid; water, glycol, diesel, gasoline, air; meets 30R2 Type 1 and 2 |
| **LOC / LOL** | Industrial and transportation; petroleum oils, glycol, water, air |

## Hydraulic Fluids

Most hydraulic fluids are one of three types:
- **Petroleum-based** — standard hydraulic oil
- **Water glycol-based**
- **Synthetic-based** — phosphate ester (fire resistant)

### The Shift to Biodegradable Fluids

The industry is switching to environmentally friendly "green" hydraulic fluids. However, many green fluids seep through ordinary hose tubes. Gates **EnviroFluid™** hoses address this with a **nitrile tube stock** that prevents permeation and handles biodegradable fluids up to **250°F (120°C)** without performance loss.

---

## Hose Storage

With proper storage, a hose can last **5–7 years on the shelf**. These environmental factors shorten shelf life:

- Temperature extremes
- Humidity
- Ozone
- Oil, solvents, corrosive materials
- Insects, rodents
- Radioactivity
- Heavy objects stacked on hose
- Sharp bends in stored hose

### Ideal Storage Conditions

- Store on a **first-in, first-out** basis
- Keep in a **cool, dry area — never exceeding +38°C (+100°F)**
- Avoid direct sunlight, rain, heaters, and electrical equipment
- **Do not crush or stack heavy objects** on hose
- Store in **original container**
- **NOTE:** If stored below freezing, warm hose up before handling, testing, or using

---

## Service Life Considerations

No hose lasts forever. Factors that shorten service life include continuous operation at:
- Maximum rated working pressure
- Maximum operating temperature
- Minimum bend radius

> **Inspect regularly.** Assemblies showing signs of wear or damage must be replaced immediately. To avoid injury or equipment damage: always ensure **proper selection**, **proper installation**, and **proper maintenance**.
