## The Three-Step Strategy

Most students who fail AWS exams skip Step 3. Don't be that student.

---

## Step 1: Complete the Digital Course

Inside Skill Builder, search for the **official exam prep course** for your chosen certification. Examples:

- *"AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner Exam Prep"* (CLF-C02)
- *"AWS Certified AI Practitioner Exam Prep"* (AIF-C01)
- *"AWS Certified Solutions Architect – Associate Exam Prep"* (SAA-C03)
- *"AWS Certified Developer – Associate Exam Prep"* (DVA-C02)

These courses are built by AWS and map directly to what's tested. They include video lessons, knowledge checks, and summaries. Plan for **8–15 hours** of content depending on the cert level.

---

## Step 2: Hands-On Practice

Reading about AWS is not the same as using it. Skill Builder gives you several ways to get practical experience:

**AWS Cloud Quest**
A role-playing game where you complete cloud missions using real AWS services. Completing the *Cloud Practitioner* quest is one of the best ways to solidify foundational knowledge before the exam.

**AWS Jam**
Scenario-based challenges where you diagnose and fix issues in a real AWS environment. Great for associate-level certifications.

**Builder Labs**
Guided, step-by-step labs that walk you through specific AWS services. Look for labs that match your certification domain list.

<Pitfall title="Don't Skip Hands-On Practice">

AWS exam questions are scenario-based — they describe a problem and ask which AWS service or approach solves it best. If you have only read about services and never used them, scenario questions are much harder. Even 2–3 hours of Cloud Quest dramatically improves exam performance.

</Pitfall>

---

## Step 3: Take the Official Practice Exam

This is the most important step — both for passing the real exam and for claiming your voucher.

In Skill Builder, search for **"Official Practice Exam"** followed by your cert name. For example:

> *"Official Practice Exam: AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner"*

The practice exam:
- Mirrors the real exam format and question style
- Provides detailed answer explanations for every question
- Is the **trigger** for generating your 100% discount voucher

<CodeLab title="Find Your Official Practice Exam">

Inside Skill Builder, use this search strategy:

1. Click **"Training Library"** in the top navigation.
2. In the search bar, type: `Official Practice Exam [your cert name]`
3. Filter by **"Digital Training"** on the left side.
4. Select the result that matches your certification exactly — verify the exam code (CLF-C02, AIF-C01, SAA-C03, or DVA-C02) is in the title.
5. Complete all questions and submit the exam.

**Do not close the browser mid-exam.** If the session times out before submission, your completion may not register.

</CodeLab>
