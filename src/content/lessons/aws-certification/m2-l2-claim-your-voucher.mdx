## How the Voucher Works

After you complete an Official Practice Exam in Skill Builder, a **100% discount voucher** is automatically generated and sent by **Pearson VUE** to your email address.

- **Delivery time:** A few days after completing the practice exam
- **Sender:** Pearson VUE (check your spam folder if it doesn't arrive)
- **Value:** 100% off one AWS certification exam (up to $300 per exam)

---

## The Rules You Must Know

### Rule 1: Maximum 4 Vouchers

You can claim one voucher per unique certification's Official Practice Exam. Complete all four practice exams across the four eligible certifications, and you unlock four vouchers — the maximum allowed per student.

### Rule 2: Each Voucher Expires in 6 Months

Every voucher is valid for **6 months from the date it is issued** — not from the date you receive it or the date you plan to test.

<Pitfall title="Don't Claim the Voucher Until You're Ready to Schedule">

The 6-month clock starts the moment the voucher is issued — which happens automatically after you complete the practice exam. If you complete the practice exam in January but are not ready to sit the real exam until August, your voucher will have expired.

**Strategy:** Complete the Official Practice Exam only when you are confident you could pass the real exam within the next few weeks. Use unofficial practice tests and course materials to prepare, then take the official practice exam as your final readiness check.

</Pitfall>

### Rule 3: One Voucher Per Exam

Each voucher can only be applied to the certification it was generated for. You cannot use a Cloud Practitioner voucher toward a Solutions Architect exam.

---

## What to Do When the Voucher Arrives

1. **Save the email** — the voucher code is in the email body. Do not delete it.
2. **Note the expiration date** — calculate your available scheduling window.
3. **Schedule your exam** within the 6-month window (instructions in the next lesson).

<Quiz question="A student completes the Official Practice Exam for Cloud Practitioner in March. They receive a voucher with a 6-month expiration. What is the latest date they can use the voucher?">

<div>

**September of the same year** (6 months from March).

The clock starts at issuance, not at the time of use. If the student does not schedule and sit the exam before September, the voucher expires — and they cannot get another one for the same certification under this program.

</div>
</Quiz>
