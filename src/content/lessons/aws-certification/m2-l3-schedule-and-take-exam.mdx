## Two Accounts, One Goal

AWS has two separate account systems — make sure you don't confuse them:

| Account | Used For |
|---|---|
| **AWS Academy / Skill Builder** | Accessing courses and practice exams |
| **AWS Certification Account** | Scheduling and managing real certification exams |

You need a **separate AWS Certification account** at `aws.training` or `certmetrics.com` to schedule your exam. If you don't have one, create it for free before proceeding.

---

## Scheduling Your Exam (Step by Step)

1. Go to your **AWS Certification Account** and log in.
2. Navigate to **"Schedule New Exam"** and select your certification.
3. Choose **Pearson VUE** as your testing provider.
4. Select your preferred format:
   - **Test center** — visit a Pearson VUE location near you (bring a valid government-issued photo ID)
   - **OnVUE (online proctoring)** — take the exam from home with your webcam, microphone, and a quiet, private room
5. Pick your date, time, and location.
6. Proceed to the **payment/checkout screen**.

---

## Applying Your Voucher Code

At the checkout screen, look for a **"Voucher or Promo Code"** field before entering any payment information.

<CodeLab title="Apply the Voucher at Checkout">

1. On the Pearson VUE checkout page, locate the field labeled **"Voucher/Promo Code"** or **"Discount Code"**.
2. Copy the voucher code exactly from the email you received (no spaces, all caps).
3. Paste it into the field and click **"Apply"**.
4. The exam price should immediately update to **$0.00**.
5. Complete the checkout — no credit card required.

**If the code doesn't apply:** double-check that you are scheduling the correct certification exam that matches the voucher. A CLF-C02 voucher will not apply to a SAA-C03 exam booking.

</CodeLab>

## Taking the Exam

**At a test center:**
- Arrive 15 minutes early
- Bring a valid, government-issued photo ID (passport or driver's license)
- Leave all personal items in a locker — no phones, notes, or smartwatches

**Online (OnVUE):**
- Test your system at least 24 hours before using the Pearson VUE system check tool
- Use a quiet, private room with a closed door
- Clear your desk completely — only your ID and a blank piece of paper (if allowed) may be present
- Ensure stable internet (wired connection preferred)
- Have your webcam and microphone working before launch

<Pitfall title="Results Are Immediate">

AWS exams show a **pass/fail result on screen** immediately after submission. Your official score report and digital badge are delivered to your AWS Certification account within a few days.

If you do not pass, you must wait **14 days** before retesting and will need a new voucher.

</Pitfall>
