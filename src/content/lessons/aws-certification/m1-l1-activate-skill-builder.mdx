## What Is AWS Skill Builder?

AWS Skill Builder is Amazon's official learning platform. Your free 12-month subscription includes:

- **600+ digital courses** across cloud, AI/ML, security, and more
- **Hands-on labs** with real AWS environments (no credit card needed)
- **Official Practice Exams** — the same question style as the real tests
- **AWS Cloud Quest** — game-based learning for hands-on practice

Thanks to the **AWS Academy Skill Builder Initiative**, CityU students enrolled in an active AWS Academy course get all of this at no cost.

---

## How to Activate (Step by Step)

1. Log in to **Canvas** and open any active AWS Academy course.
2. Click the **Modules** tab in the left sidebar.
3. Scroll to the **"AWS T&C Resources"** module.
4. Click **"Activate Your AWS Skill Builder Offer"**.
5. Complete the consent/survey form and submit.
6. **Check your email.** Within a few business days, AWS Skill Builder will send login instructions to your Canvas-registered email address.

<Pitfall title="Email Not Verified in Canvas?">

If your Canvas email is unverified, the system will not register your eligibility — and you will not receive the Skill Builder invitation. Before activating, go to **Canvas → Account → Settings** and confirm your email address is verified.

</Pitfall>

---

## What Happens Next?

Once you receive the Skill Builder email, click the link to set up your account. Keep that email — it contains your login details and subscription confirmation.

You are now ready to pick your certification path.
