## Meet Your Instructor

This lesson introduces the instructor and sets expectations for the course. Understanding who is teaching you — their background, experience, and approach — helps you calibrate how to engage with the material and what lens they bring to Information Systems.

---

## What to Watch For

As you watch, take note of:

- The instructor's professional and academic background
- Their perspective on Information Systems as a discipline
- How they connect theory to real-world practice
- Any recommended resources or supplementary materials they mention

---

## Why Instructor Background Matters

Information Systems sits at the intersection of business and technology. Instructors who have worked in industry bring practitioner insights that textbooks don't capture — specific tools used on the job, common mistakes organizations make, and the gap between how IS is taught and how it is actually implemented.
